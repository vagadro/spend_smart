Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 2026

1. Data We Collect

When you use Spendly, we collect the following information to provide the service:

  • Account information — your name and email address provided at registration
  • Expense data — amounts, categories, dates, and descriptions you enter
  • Usage data — pages visited and actions taken within the app

We do not collect payment information, government IDs, or any sensitive financial credentials.

2. How We Use Your Data

The data we collect is used solely to operate and improve Spendly. Specifically:

  • To provide and maintain your personal expense tracking dashboard
  • To authenticate your account and keep it secure
  • To generate summaries and reports based on your expense entries
  • To diagnose technical issues and improve the service

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal data to any third party.

3. Data Storage

Your data is stored on secure servers. We take reasonable technical and organisational measures to protect your information against unauthorised access, alteration, or loss.

If you delete your account, your personal data and expense records will be removed from our systems within 30 days, except where retention is required by applicable law.

4. Third-Party Services

Spendly may use third-party services to help operate the platform, such as hosting providers and analytics tools. These parties are given access only to the data necessary to perform their functions and are contractually obligated to keep it confidential.

We do not integrate with advertising networks or share your data for marketing purposes.

5. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy or how your data is handled, please reach out to us at the contact details available on our website. We will respond to all privacy-related enquiries within 7 business days.

You have the right to request access to, correction of, or deletion of any personal data we hold about you at any time.
